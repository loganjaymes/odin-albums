Selfish Machines

Description

Selfish Machines is the sophomore album by the band Pierce the Veil, released on June 22, 2010. Vic Fuentes, the band's frontman and rhythm guitarist, wrote a majority of the songs, and co-wrote a handful of them with Tom Denney, a former member of A Day to Remember.

Alongside Fuentes, Jeremy McKinnon of the previously mentioned A Day to Remember featured on the song "Caraphernelia", becoming one of the band's biggest songs to date, and getting a music video as a result.

The album itself is a blend of post-hardcore, emo, alternative, and, at times, latin music. The prior three genres are typical for the band, however the final is most notable due to its relation, or lackthereof, to the former. This comes as a direct result to the members themselves, as a majority of them come from latin, more specifically Mexican, heritage. This is especially seen in songs such as "Besitos", of which is a Spanish phrase that literally translates to "kisses".
